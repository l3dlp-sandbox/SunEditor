## 3.2.1

### Bugfix

* Fixed a bug where pressing Enter while composing text with an IME (e.g. Korean) on iOS browsers with a physical keyboard moved the last composed character to the next line. [#1654](https://github.com/JiHong88/suneditor/issues/1654)
* Fixed a bug where pressing Tab could move focus to a hidden off-screen element, making the cursor disappear.
